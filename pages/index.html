Will Anybody Ever Be A Better Shooter Than Stephen Curry?

Stephen Curry has the greatest shooting resume of all time. Adding to that list is the all time three point record passing Ray Allen. Below is the Steph Curry tenure report.

  • Most 3's in NBA History
  • 3 NBA Championships
  • 2 MVP's (1st Ever Unanimous MVP)
  • Most 3's in Playoffs All Time

With this high level of production from Stephen Curry its hard to think that anyone will ever come close to the amount of success that he has afforded for himself. He's also on pace to end his career with 4000 threes. A number we may never see touched from any player for the rest of time.
